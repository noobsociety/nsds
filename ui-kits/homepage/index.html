Why

Not a card grid. A world.

For developers, designers, and makers who want a portfolio to discover, not only read.

  • The site is a world

    Pages are maps, links are interactables. Clicks move your character and open overlays — not a new tab.

    Spatial

  • One typed bridge

    A map object in Phaser fires the matching React overlay. Spatial play and UI keep a clear separation.

    Live

  • Sprite-forward

    Clear silhouettes, readable at a glance. Space, objects, and character designed to read as one coherent portfolio.

    Personal

  • Built in the open

    CI on every push. Cloudflare Pages deploy. Milestones, issues, and devblog — all visible.

    Open
